Diet Plan for ARTHRITIS

BEST FOOD TO HAVE IF YOU HAVE ARTHRITIS:

  1. Fatty Fish
  2. Garlic
  3. Ginger
  4. Broccoli
  5. Walnuts
  6. Berries
  7. Spinach

FOOD TO AVOID EATING IF YOU HAVE ARTHRITIS:

  1. Added Sugar
  2. Processed and red meats
  3. Gluten-containing foods
  4. Foods high in salt
  5. Highly processed foods
  6. Alcohol
  7. Certain vegetable oils
  8. Foods high in AGEs
